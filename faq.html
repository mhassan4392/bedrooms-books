FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I have to pay early monthly payments or security deposits?

Absolutely not, Stanza Semplice ® applies the Zero Deposit ® formula to all its students . You will not have to pay any security deposit, you will be asked for advance monthly payments

Is the contract in solidarity with the other tenants of the house?

Contracts with Room Simple ® are individual. Each tenant has his contract with his fee, the other rooms do not affect your contractual position in any way.

Stanza Semplice ® is a real estate agency?

No, Stanza Semplice ® manages only single rooms for university students all over Italy, directly. It does not carry out intermediation activities, in fact the contract you will do it directly with Stanza Semplice ® of the reference city and you will not have to pay any mediation.

Are there any consumption adjustments?

There are no adjustments on consumption, however if disproportionate consumption occurs for the apartment a balance can be requested on the relative bills. There are pre-established parameters to be respected.

What is the duration of the contract?

The contracts follow the university cycle and in most cases have a duration of 12 months.

Can I cancel early in the contract?

Of course, during the course of your contract, you will be able to give early notice with a predetermined notice. Stanza Semplice ® will help you find a replacement right away.

I have a problem and I have to leave the room immediately. How can I do?

Room Simple ® will activate immediately to find a substitute to take your room as soon as possible, so svincolarti the contract.

In my previous experiences I didn't have a contact person and when I had a problem I didn't know who to call.

By joining the Club Stanza Semplice ® you can count on your Apartment Manager, to which you can refer for anything during your stay in the room.

The Simple bedroom apartments ® are all the same?

The apartments are renovated and uniformly furnished, following the directions and suggestions of our students in every Italian university city. Every detail is designed to meet the needs of the student.

Am I entitled to the conventions as part of the club room Simple ® ?

Of course, in every city by presenting your personal card of the Club Room Simple ® you can enjoy different conventions in various public venues.

I have read that the Stanza Semplice ® club exists . What is it?

The Club Stanza Semplice ® has been created in order to guarantee our customers an exclusive 360 ​​° service thanks to which you can benefit from innumerable advantageous services, such as maintenance included in the price.

Is it possible to have monthly payment invoices?

Of course, Stanza Semplice ® issues invoices on a monthly basis.
